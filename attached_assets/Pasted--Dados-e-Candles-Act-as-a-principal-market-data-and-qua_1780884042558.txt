5. Modo Agressivo e Stacking
Act as a principal quantitative portfolio and campaign-allocation engineer.

Audit aggressive VST stacking across all configured symbols.

Entry 1 is exploratory. Entries 2–10 require:

- same direction;
- persistent or stronger calibrated edge;
- low enough uncertainty;
- new completed candle or meaningful state change;
- cooldown;
- available campaign and portfolio capacity;
- no duplicate signal or market event;
- acceptable campaign drawdown.

Prohibit martingale, loss doubling and blind averaging down.

Measure marginal results for every stacking depth:

- incremental PnL;
- marginal expectancy;
- additional drawdown;
- edge at insertion;
- contribution to campaign outcome;
- MFE and MAE;
- correlation-adjusted exposure.

Create control groups:

- one entry;
- maximum three entries;
- maximum five entries;
- aggressive maximum ten entries.

Prove whether each additional depth adds value out of sample.
